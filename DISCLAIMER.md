# Disclaimer

This project is for training, research, and software verification only.

It is not an operational ATC system, not a navigation product, not a certified simulator, and not a safety-critical decision-support tool.

Do not use this project for:

- Real-world air traffic control
- Aircraft navigation
- Dispatch or flight operations
- Regulatory compliance
- Certification or proficiency sign-off
- Emergency or contingency operations

The simulator may contain simplified, derived, approximate, incomplete, or stale data. Even when a validation script passes, that only means the repository's current deterministic checks passed. It does not mean the data or behavior is operationally correct, legally authoritative, or approved for real-world use.

Official aeronautical publications, regulator-approved manuals, and authorized operational systems remain the only valid sources for real-world operations.
